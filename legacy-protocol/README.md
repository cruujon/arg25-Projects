2. Public Baton Line (Anonymous Ideological Relay),

Users can also leave anonymous, onchain "thought capsules" ‚Äî short statements, ideas, or reflections meant to be discovered and carried forward by future generations. Each relay passes the "baton" of an idea to the next participant, forming a visible lineage of influence while the message content itself remains encrypted. Over time, this creates a semantic web of inheritance ‚Äî a living archive of human thought that evolves across identities and epochs.
